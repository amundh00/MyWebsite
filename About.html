My Story

The Start

My love for making videos started at a young age. When I was in 6th grade, I started videorecording myself on the trampoline and biking. And it all has evolved from there. I also started to play video games at a young age and are still partially involved in that. I always wondered how it was made and has a facination for technology.

Pre-Highschool

When I started junior high school, I choose “Media and communication“ as my electives. From the first day I knew I would try to enter into the business of Media and communications at High School as well.

Highschool

In Highschool, I did a lot of interesting and fun projects. My and a friend make a lot of car videos for the car community in Trondheim. And I also became a finalist and won one of the main prizes in a European film festival held in Portugal.

University in Lillehammer

After Highschool, I attended a one-year study in Lillehammer, Norway. The focus was on “Audivisuelle medier“. Where we learned a lot about film theory, and the Norwegian film, TV, and game industry.

University in Bergen, 3D and Animations

And when I found the 3D and Animations study in Bergen I knew that was the one for me. From making a Lego figure to learning the basics of modeling. To realistic renders, character rigging, and game development.

Programming

In 2023 i started my study in front-end development. Programming is something that i have always wanted to learn, but it seemed a bit scary. my father has always been a programmer and tryed to influence me to start programming. And i have always had the urge to start. And finaly i have! Building this website is one of the first projects i have had outside of school.

The Future

I am excited to see what comes next, and what I will make in the future.
