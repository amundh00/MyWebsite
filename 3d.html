What is VFX?

VFX is a lot of different tools and techniques. You can use VFX as a tool to make a scene more extreme or “cool“. VFX is also a tool to use in a lot of other creative work, such as advertisements or social media videos to keep the viewer interested in what they are watching. Most modern movies and most of comercials has several scenes made with VFX.
